Technical Portfolio

Of the past projects I have completed, one that gave me some trouble but also ended up being a very interesting project to work on was a Seam Carving program. The process of Seam Carving is a program that is able to find the most important, or parts most people would care about the most, parts of an image and allow for someone to rescale their image with minimal warping. It does this by finding some seams, or paths of pixels that are deemed unimportant and either removes or adds to them depending on how the user wants to resize the image. This allows for images to be resized with different ratios from the original image and minimizes how distorted they get. While difficult in the beginning, starting to understand the process and how to code it out greatly helped me learn how to think about and process much larger coding assignments than I was used to.
